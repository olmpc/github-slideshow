Psychic Prejudice

Album drops 2/22/2022

Features confrontational confections. Bouncy but brooding, the forthcoming collection organizes 3-minute themes telling truths of the night.

Fall along with those native to The Night. Psychic Prejudice chronicles the stickily sweet fun-chute, the mainline to Inverno. Begin as a flesh and blood man haplessly in for a terrible surprise a la Las Vegas, only to finish as a walking crystal doomed to feed forever. Frozen into a counterfeit youth, the lost boys of The Holoworld rise from The Neverlands below to gavotte around like royalty among normies.
